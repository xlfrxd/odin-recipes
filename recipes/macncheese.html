Mac 'n' Cheese

Description

This is a simple way to create Mac and Cheese at home this summer.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Bowl
  • Macaroni

Steps

  1. Heat water in bowl
  2. Put macaroni in boiling water
  3. Drain macaroni when cooked
  4. Put cheese in bowl
  5. Mix macaroni and cheese
